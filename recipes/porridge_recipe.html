Best Porridge

Quino Porridge

Best in the streets. Energy giving and nutritious work of art

Made from Quinoa and Sorghumflour

Ingredients

  • Quinoa flour 1 cup
  • Sorghum flour 1 cup
  • Sugar
  • 2 ounces of Ghee
  • Half cup table sugar

Recipe

  1. Assemble the ingredients
  2. Preheat a pan with 3 cups of water
  3. Pre mixing the ingredients in a jug
  4. Add the mixture to the boiling water in the pan
  5. Stir until it has a thick but flowy consistency
  6. Let it cook until you smell the aroma
  7. Serve while hot
