Scrambled Eggs

Best scrambled eggs in the south of the Sahara

Cooked to perfection

Ingredients

  • 3 free range eggs
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Coriander Leaves

Scrambled eggs recipe

  1. Assemeble your ingredients and cooking utensils
  2. Beat the eggs to aerate them
  3. Dice the onions tomatoes and corriander
  4. Heat the butter and saute the onions until golden brown
  5. Add the diced tomatoes and cooked to a fine paste
  6. under low heat add the egg and some salt and cook under low heat while srambling
  7. Add the diced coriander leaves and cook for a minute
  8. Serve while hot
